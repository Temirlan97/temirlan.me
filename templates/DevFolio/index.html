About me

If I rewind and look back at my life, I’ve taken paths I never thought I would and met people I think I never deserved. Being born and raised as just another child, I’ve lived the life that significantly differs from lives of those surrounding me during my childhood. As known from geometry, small change of angle in the beginning results in huge displacement in the end, hence it makes sense to start my story from the very beginning to give full image of who am I and why am I.

Since very early ages I have a passion for making something new, let it be writing poems, drawing, shooting movies or implementing a new software. If you watch through my life, it will look like becoming a software engineer was my destiny. I never had big passion towards science itself, but due my successful performance in state and national Physics Olympiads, I became a part of national team and have been representing my country on various international Physics Olympiads. During those years, I’ve strongly developed my problem solving and critical thinking skills. After graduating from high-school, I have started studying Electrical Engineering, because it was something I had some knowledge in and something that is not Physics (I was tired of Physics by that time). Due to university policies and course obligations, I ended up taking Computer Science module as well. And I loved it and switched my major to CS not long after, since passion for creating and good problem solving skills are almost everything one needs to fall in love with programming.
